About

Thanks for checking out my blog. I'm Nathan Wallace, a Ruby developer living in Cleveland with my beautiful wife Claire and two dogs Oliver and Penny.

I began with Ruby and Rails in 2012 when my aunt asked me about making an Excel spreadsheet to track inventory and expenses for her business. It sounded like a perfect candidate for a web app, but I didn't know how to build one on my own. I searched around for good open-source tools to get the job done and eventually decided on Rails. I taught myself Ruby and Rails in my spare time until I got a job doing Rails full-time in 2013.

I'm a big believer in self-improvement, and it's the open-source community that makes this possible. I started this blog to do my part to give back. I hope it helps someone out there!

I'd love to hear from you, so please feel free to contact me by email or on Twitter.
